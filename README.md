# creating meaningful metrics for a debate nonprofit
i measured the impact of a debate nonprofit (Brooklyn Debate League) on school representation in the nyc debate circuit.
fully filtered & broke down NYC DOE public datasets (largest school district in the country) by school type. first included, then excluded teams that the nonprofit created. this allowed them to measure their impact and inform next expansion steps.
aka, me taking advantage of a final class assignment to research something real-world i care about...
